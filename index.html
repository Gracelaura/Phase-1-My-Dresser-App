Swanky Outfit

If you want to go for very expensive and fashionable attire, in a way that is intended to attract people's attention and admiration, then swanky is the way to go.Simply achieve this by keeping things polished with clean lines, polished accessories and neat hair and makeup.

Chic Outfit

Interested in keeping up with current trends in an elegant, smart and sophisticated manner? Go chic. Chic fashion style is often marked by luxurious fabrics that elevate your closet to new levels. They are lasting, classic and will be dependable staple pieces in your wardrobe for years to come. Think of chic style as an uncomplicated look put together with little or no effort.
